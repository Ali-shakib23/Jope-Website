About Us

About us


Jope connects visual impaired impaired people who are looking for jobs or training with companies that are searching for them. Our vision is to insure that every visual impaired in the world have a suitable job.



Our Mission


To help the visual impaired to meet the market needs and to make them feel as effective people in their community as well as achieving the SDG goals and motivating them
